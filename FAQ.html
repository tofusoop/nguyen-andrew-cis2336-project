What is Art Connect?

Art Connect is a platform for artists to showcase their work and for art enthusiasts to discover new talent.

How do I submit my artwork?

You can submit your artwork by navigating to the "Submit Art" page and filling out the submission form.

What types of artwork can I submit?

You can submit various types of artwork including paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital art.

Is there a fee to submit artwork?

No, submitting artwork on Art Connect is free of charge.

How can I contact Art Connect for support?

You can contact us through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@artconnect.com.
